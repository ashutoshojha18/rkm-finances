Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our loans and financial services

What types of loans does RKM Finances provide?

+

We provide a comprehensive range of loans including home loans, personal loans, business loans, education loans, project finance, machinery loans, and working capital solutions.

What is the typical processing time for loan approval?

+

Processing time varies depending on the type of loan and documentation. Generally, personal and business loans can be processed within 3-7 working days, while project finance may take 2-4 weeks.

Do you provide services outside North India?

+

Yes, while we have strong presence in North India, Mumbai, and Indore, we are expanding our services across India. Contact us to check availability in your location.

What documents are required for loan applications?

+

Document requirements vary by loan type. Common documents include identity proof, address proof, income documents, and business registration documents. Our team will provide a detailed checklist based on your specific needs.

Do you provide insurance services as well?

+

Yes, we offer both corporate and personal insurance services in addition to our comprehensive loan and financial advisory services.
